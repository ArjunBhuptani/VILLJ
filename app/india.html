Using trusted lending to crowdfund solar panels

Trusted lending circles are a cultural norm in South Asia. Participants periodically buy into a central pool of money which is then redistributed based on need. Because of their structure, they have a very low default rate and have been shown to be effective for funding community projects in rural areas.
